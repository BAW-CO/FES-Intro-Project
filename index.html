Here are some of my projects:

  • Bugette: Baked Goods on a Mission

    Logo Suite and Submarks, Brand Identity, Package Design, Brand Touchpoints, Website Design, Ad Design, Social Media Templates

    Cricket products are quickly being recognized for their sustainability and nutritious value. The other cricket products I've seen on the market look sciency, which isn't bad — but I wanted to make cricket protein cute. I wanted to take cricket flour and wrap it in European nostalgia while showing off all of it's amazing world-saving qualities. I wanted to develop a brand of cricket flour that would fit in but stand out on the shelves. Bugette is my answer to that. It's cozy, it's yummy, it's fresh baked goods on a mission.

  • Hinder: A Satirical Dating App

    Branding, UI/UX Design, Brand Touchpoints

    Hinder is a daring anti-hero. It's not for everyone, this app is for risk-takers, adventurers, and yes, the hopeless romantics out there. You might not find true love, but you may find an opportunity to discover common ground with a stranger. And couldn't the world use a little more of that?

  • Adventure Pro Magazine
